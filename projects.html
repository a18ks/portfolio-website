Simon-game

Simon is a memory-based electronic game where players are required to repeat a sequence of lights and sounds in the correct order. The game begins with a simple pattern, and each round, the sequence gets longer, challenging the player to remember and replicate it. The sequence is shown by flashing colored buttons, each producing a unique sound. Players must press the buttons in the same order as shown to continue. If the player presses an incorrect button, the game ends. Simon helps enhance memory, focus, and reaction time, making it a fun and engaging challenge.

Interactive-Quiz-Application

The Interactive Quiz Application is a fun and engaging web-based quiz tool that allows users to answer multiple-choice questions on various topics. With a user-friendly interface, it displays questions one at a time and tracks the user's score in real-time. The app includes a timer for added challenge, and once the quiz is completed, it provides feedback on the user's performance. Users can retry the quiz to improve their scores. Designed with smooth animations and interactive features, this quiz app offers an enjoyable and educational experience.

To-do-list

The To-Do List application is a simple yet powerful tool for managing tasks and staying organized. Users can add, edit, delete, and mark tasks as complete with a clean, intuitive interface. The app supports task filtering (all, active, completed) and stores tasks in localStorage, ensuring they persist across sessions. With smooth animations for adding and removing tasks, it offers an enjoyable experience while keeping users productive. Whether for personal use or team collaboration, the To-Do List app helps users stay on top of their responsibilities efficiently.
